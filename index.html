A Cosmic Smart Contracting Platform

Straightedge is a general-purpose WASM smart contracting platform built using the Cosmos SDK and CosmWasm.

The network is governed and secured by the holders of the STR token, which is widely distributed as a modified hard spoon of the Edgeware genesis lockdrop.

Straightedge will launch on 15 Sep 2020.
